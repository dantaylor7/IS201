Profits Per State

This visual representation depicts the sum of sales for the Global Superstore. Sales vary across a range of product categories and are divided in this visual representation by state. Hover over a state to examine the summary details for sales. Top of page




Profitable State Review

This data visualization depicts the 2013 and 2014 fiscal years of sales in California. This is the summary of profits from all product categories. The sum of profits can be viewed for each month between this two year time period. Users can also filter in the 2011 and 2012 fiscal years if desired. Top of page




Profitable State Category Analysis

This data visualization shows a summary of the sum of proftis for each product category in the state of California. California was the the state with the highest sum of profits for the Global Superstore. Viewers can also filter which categories they want to appear on this visual. Top of page




Profitable Category Forecast

The accessories category produced the highest sum of profits among all other product categories in the state of California. This data visualization depics all recorded profits for the accessories section in California as well as forecasts profits for the following year using statistical analysis.
